Creator OS by Matthew Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

Overview

Creator OS by Matthew is used to manage Matthew Carvalho's own creator accounts and content workflows.

Data Access

The app may access basic profile information and video publishing permissions only when authorized by the account owner through platform OAuth flows.

How Data Is Used

Data is used only to prepare, upload, and manage creator content for Matthew's authorized accounts. Data is not sold or shared with third parties.

Storage

Authentication tokens and app credentials are stored locally by the owner and are not published on this website.

Contact

For privacy questions about this app, contact Matthew Carvalho.
