Creator OS by Matthew

Creator OS by Matthew is a private desktop dashboard used by Matthew Carvalho to prepare, organize, and publish video content across his owned creator channels.

Terms of Service Privacy Policy

Purpose

The tool helps manage upload readiness, captions, hashtags, cover-frame choices, video metadata, and creator workflow handoffs for Matthew's own content.

TikTok Integration

  • Login Kit lets Matthew authorize his own TikTok accounts through TikTok OAuth.
  • Content Posting API is used to upload finished video files to authorized accounts for review and posting.
  • The app is not a public posting service and does not automate likes, follows, messages, comments, or engagement.
